Newbie Companion

Getting into a new sport is tough

The rules of cricket, in particular, are especially confusing to those who did not grow up watching the sport. With more than 2.5 billion devoted followers across the globe, cricket has the second largest fanbase of any sport. This means that someone who does not understand the rules is missing out on the ability to connect with a third of the global population. Our app helps you to learn and follow the game by providng contextual information along with a play by play commentary for both live games and completed games. With our app you can quickly familarize yourself with cricket and join the billions in no time.
